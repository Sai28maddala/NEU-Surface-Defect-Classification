Surface Defect Types

Crazing

Interconnected or adjacent cracks in a network. Often caused by surface cooling too rapidly.

Inclusion

Foreign particles embedded in the metal matrix during the manufacturing process.

Patches

Regions with different surface textures, often due to uneven cooling or material inconsistencies.

Pitted Surface

Small, crater-like depressions in the surface, often caused by corrosion or material removal.

Rolled-in Scale

Scale or oxide pressed into the surface during the rolling process.

Scratches

Linear marks caused by friction between the material and another object during handling or processing.
